Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long are the lessons? Our lessons typically run for one hour per session, with flexible scheduling to accommodate your family's needs.
  • Do we need any prior experience? Not at all! Our program is designed for complete beginners. We provide all the materials and step-by-step instruction to get you started.
  • What materials do I need to bring? We provide all necessary materials including yarn, needles, and hooks for each lesson. Just bring yourselves and a willingness to learn!
  • Can I purchase custom items? Yes! We're happy to discuss custom orders for specific colors, sizes, or styles. Contact us to share your ideas.
  • Do you offer gift wrapping? We offer complimentary gift wrapping for all handmade items to make your purchase extra special.
  • What's your return policy? Since all items are handmade with care, we accept returns within 14 days for unused items in original condition. Contact us to initiate a return.
  • Can adults take classes without a child? Our Stitching Generations program is specifically designed for grandparent-grandchild pairs, but we're exploring adult-only workshops. Contact us to express your interest!
